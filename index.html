Pre-Inspection Preparation

To make the inspection process as smooth as possible, consider the following preparation steps:

  • Clear Access to Electrical Panels: Ensure that all electrical panels and fuse boxes are easily accessible. This allows our electricians to perform a thorough check without any obstructions.
  • Prepare for Safety Checks: Our team will assess your home for potential safety hazards. Clear away any items or furniture that might obstruct access to outlets, switches, or wiring.
  • Document Recent Changes: If you’ve made any recent electrical upgrades, repairs, or renovations, it’s helpful to provide our team with details about these changes. This information can assist in a more accurate assessment.
  • Test Electrical Outlets and Fixtures: Before the inspection, test your outlets and fixtures to ensure they’re functioning correctly. Report any issues to our team so we can address them promptly.
  • Review Electrical Codes: Familiarize yourself with local electrical codes and standards. Our team will check for compliance with these codes during the inspection.

What to Expect During the Inspection

  1. Initial Assessment: Our Electrician Auckland will begin with an initial assessment of your home’s electrical system. This includes a visual inspection of panels, wiring, outlets, and fixtures.
  2. Detailed Inspection: We’ll conduct a detailed inspection of your electrical system, including testing circuits, evaluating grounding and bonding, and checking for any signs of wear or damage.
  3. Safety Evaluation: Our priority is your safety. We’ll evaluate your home for potential electrical hazards such as overloaded circuits, faulty wiring, and improper grounding.
  4. Code Compliance Check: We’ll verify that your electrical system complies with local codes and regulations. This includes checking for any outdated or non-compliant installations.
  5. Recommendations and Reporting: After the inspection, we’ll provide you with a comprehensive report detailing our findings and recommendations. If any issues are identified, we’ll suggest appropriate solutions and discuss the next steps with you.
  6. Follow-Up: If any repairs or upgrades are needed, our team at Strike Electrical can schedule follow-up visits to address these issues promptly and efficiently.
